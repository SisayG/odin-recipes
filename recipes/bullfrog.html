Bullfrog Cocktail

Description

A supercharged version of a Long Island Iced Tea. With four different liquors and an energy drink, this strong cocktail recipe is enough to keep you going all night long.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. (30ml) Vodka
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Rum
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Blue Curaçao
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Red Bull
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Tequilla
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Gin
  • 1 oz. (30ml) Lemon Juice
  • Garnish: Lemon Slice/Cherry

Steps

  1. Add ice to tall glass and pour clear liquors inside
  2. Add lemon juice and drizzle blue curaçao over top.
  3. Top with red bull and garnish with a lemon wedge and a cherry
